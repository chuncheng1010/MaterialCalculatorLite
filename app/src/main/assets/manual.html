How to Use Calculator

Step 1 - Select your measurement system that you will be using to measure from the drop down menu at the top of the screen by pressing the arrow to display your selection:
  • Metric
  • Imperial
Step 2 - Select the type of material you would like to calculate the volume for from the drop down menu at the top of the screen by pressing the arrow to display your selection.

Step 3 - Select the shape you would like to calculate the volume for from the drop down menu at the top of the screen by pressing the arrow to display your selection.

Step 4 - Enter your Inputs for the shape.

Step 5 - Press the "Calculate Answers" button for the calculator to calculate your answer.

Step 6 - The answers are able to be saved to the folders or the calculator can be reset to start again instantly by pressing the refresh button located in the overflow.
